StudySphere is a centralized academic planning system designed to help college students reclaim their time. By combining workload visualization with proactive academic monitoring, StudySphere helps students stay organized, improve their performance, and avoid the burnout often associated with heavy course loads.

🚀 Core Mission
The primary purpose of StudySphere is to ensure students stay on top of their assignments through strong task visibility, clear prioritization, and proactive reminders. Following client feedback, the platform has pivoted to a dashboard-centered architecture that eliminates excessive navigation, putting deadlines and priorities front and center.

✨ Key Features
📊 Centralized Dashboard
Course Panels: Side-by-side visualization of all enrolled courses, inspired by the intuitive layout of platforms like Canvas.

Instant Visibility: View upcoming assignments, deadlines, and incomplete work across all classes from a single screen.

📝 Assignment & Task Management
Prioritization: Automated systems to highlight which tasks require immediate attention.

Deadline Awareness: Proactive reminders to ensure no assignment is missed.

Study Session Logging: Track the time spent on specific subjects to better understand workload distribution.

🛡️ Academic Wellness
Burnout Alerts: Monitoring tools that notify students when their workload reaches high-stress levels.

GPA Monitoring: Real-time academic performance insights to keep students on track with their long-term goals.

Workload Visualization: Data-driven insights into how a student's time is being utilized.

🛠️ Project Evolution
StudySphere was refined through iterative client interviews to move away from deep menu nesting and toward a high-visibility dashboard model. This ensures that the most critical functions—assignment tracking and deadline awareness—are always the highest priority.
